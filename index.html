About Us

Urban Haven Apartments is your go-to choice for comfortable, affordable, and modern living spaces in the serene surroundings of Murang’a, Kiharu. With over 12 years of experience in the rental housing industry, we have built a solid reputation for providing well-maintained and thoughtfully designed apartments tailored to fit a variety of lifestyles and budgets. Our journey began with a simple mission: to create a community where everyone feels at home, regardless of their stage in life. From students to working professionals, couples to families, we are committed to offering a diverse range of living spaces that cater to your unique needs. We pride ourselves on our excellent customer service, attention to detail, and our dedication to providing a welcoming and safe environment for all our residents. At Urban Haven Apartments, we understand that finding the right place to live is more than just about securing a roof over your head—it’s about becoming part of a community that values comfort, convenience, and quality living. This is why our apartments come equipped with modern amenities such as free Wi-Fi, on-site laundry services, parking, a gym, and a swimming pool, designed to enhance your living experience. We also believe in creating spaces where residents can connect and unwind, which is why we offer communal features like our community theatre and serene outdoor areas. Our location in Kiharu offers easy access to essential services, schools, shopping centers, and public transportation, making it a practical and hassle-free choice for your next home. We are now taking our business online to make it even easier for you to explore and secure the perfect living space. Through our website, you can browse through available apartments, learn more about our amenities, and even book a viewing at your convenience. As we continue to grow and innovate, our commitment remains the same: to provide you with a welcoming, affordable, and modern living experience that you can truly call home. At Urban Haven Apartments, you’re not just a tenant—you’re part of a community. We invite you to be part of our journey and experience a new standard of living tailored just for you. Welcome to Urban Haven Apartments—where your comfort and happiness are our top priority.

Amenities

Stay connected and productive with Urban Haven Apartments’ complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi. Whether you're working from home, streaming your favorite shows, or staying in touch with family and friends, our reliable internet ensures you’re always connected. Please note that while Wi-Fi is available to most residents, those living in single rooms will need to make alternative arrangements for internet access.

Swimming Pool

Cool off and relax in our luxurious swimming pool, available to all Urban Haven residents. Whether you want to swim laps for exercise or simply lounge by the water, our well-maintained pool offers a refreshing escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life. Dive into relaxation whenever you need it

COMMUNITY THEATRE

Immerse yourself in entertainment and social activities at our community theatre. This space is perfect for movie nights, events, or simply gathering with neighbors for a shared experience. Available to all residents, the community theatre brings people together and offers a unique way to unwind after a busy day, helping to create a vibrant and connected community at Urban Haven Apartments.

ELEVATOR ACCESS

Navigating the upper floors of Urban Haven Apartments is made effortless with our elevator service. Designed for both comfort and convenience, the elevator ensures easy access to all floors, making it especially useful for families, seniors, and residents with mobility challenges. Enjoy the ease of movement within your building, no matter which floor you live on.

GYM

Health and fitness are just steps away from your front door. Our state-of-the-art gym is equipped with modern machines and free weights, offering everything you need to stay fit and active. Open to all residents except those in single rooms and bedsitters, the gym is a perfect way to maintain a healthy lifestyle right at home.

ON SITE LAUNDRY

Say goodbye to the hassle of laundromats and off-site laundry services. Urban Haven Apartments provides an affordable on-site laundry facility where you can easily wash and dry your clothes for just Kshs.150 per basket. This convenient service allows you to take care of your laundry without leaving the comfort of your residence.

FREE WATER

At Urban Haven Apartments, we believe that basic utilities should be hassle-free. That’s why we offer free water to all residents, ensuring that your daily needs are met without the worry of additional costs. Whether you’re using it for cooking, cleaning, or just staying hydrated, we’ve got you covered with unlimited access to clean, safe water.
