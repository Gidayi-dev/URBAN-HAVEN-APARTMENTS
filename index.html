About Us

Welcome to Urban Haven Apartments, your go-to choice for comfortable, affordable, and modern living spaces in Murang’a, Kiharu. With over 12 years of experience in the rental housing industry, we’ve built a strong reputation for providing quality, well-maintained apartments tailored to fit a variety of lifestyles and budgets. Whether you’re a student, working professional, couple, or family, our diverse range of living spaces—from cozy single rooms to spacious two-bedroom self-contained apartments—ensures you’ll find a place to call home. At Urban Haven Apartments, we don’t just offer a roof over your head—we offer a community. Our apartments come with a host of amenities, including free Wi-Fi, on-site parking, a gym, a swimming pool, and a community theatre, all designed to enhance your living experience. Located in the serene surroundings of Kiharu, we offer easy access to essential services, ensuring that your stay with us is convenient and hassle-free. As we move online, we are committed to making it even easier for you to explore and secure your ideal living space. Whether you're looking for a cozy single room or a spacious family apartment, Urban Haven Apartments is here to meet your needs. We invite you to browse through our available spaces and book a viewing today. Welcome to a new standard of living. Welcome to Urban Haven
